Tentative Topic: NYC Housing Data Analysis

We can look at housing development, renters and their homes, homeowners and their homes, and New Yorker demographics and their neighborhoods. This broad scope allows us to narrow to specific topics or questions we want to answer.

Possible Research Questions:

  • What neighborhoods can a new renter/homebuyer purchase a home with $XYZ amount?
  • What are the drivers of real estate prices in NYC/neighborhood?
  • Based on an individual's socioeconomic background, where are they likely to rent/buy and for how much?
  • What neighborhoods are seeing growth in affordable housing?

Data Sources:

  • Furman Center - State of the City
  • CoreData.nyc
  • NYC Open Data - Housing
  • NYC Comptroller - Affordability Index
  • NYS Comptroller Report 17-2024
  • Columbia Poverty Center - Housing Affordability
  • Rent Guidelines Board - 2024 Study
  • Department of Labor
  • American Community Survey
